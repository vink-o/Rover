============================== MISSION BRIEFING ==============================

Year 2047

For centuries, humanity has gazed at the red planet in the night sky,
wondering what secrets lie hidden beneath its endless deserts of dust
and stone. Mars has always been a symbol of curiosity, exploration,
and the unknown.

After decades of orbital missions and robotic probes, humanity has
finally taken the next step.

A new exploration rover has landed safely on the Martian surface.

=============================== YOUR ROLE ===================================

You are the remote operator guiding this rover from Earth.
Every command travels millions of kilometers through space.
Every decision you make determines whether the mission succeeds or fails.

=========================== MISSION OBJECTIVES ==============================

  - Navigate the dangerous Martian terrain
  - Locate and scan scientific samples
  - Manage the rover's limited energy supply
  - Avoid rocks and environmental hazards
  - Return safely to the base station

=============================== WARNING ====================================

Mars is an unforgiving world.
Energy is limited.
The terrain is unpredictable.
One mistake could end the mission forever.

If the rover loses power, it will remain silent on the Martian surface.
